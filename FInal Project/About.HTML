What is Minecraft about?

Minecraft is a sandbox game where the only limitation is your imagination. At its core Minecraft is a survival game where the nights and caves can be a challenge. The main purpose of the game Though is that of building and adventure. In its pure form Minecraft is an infinite bucket of Legos.

Mods

Since its creation Minecraft has had countless Mods(modifications) to the base game. from new animales to new blocks the community finds ways to use the creativity to make the game more enjoyable for all.
