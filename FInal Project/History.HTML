History Of Minecraft:

When starting out the game was updated monthly as the devs (Notch and Jens) along with help from many others began the creation of the now top sandbox game. This game picked up popularity almos instantly becoming a succes. Though this succes lead to Mojang Studios being able to continue it also lead to a large amount of popularity. This popularity eventualy came back to haunt notch wishing he could make an update withi\out thousand of questions. Evenetually Notch sold the game to the multi Billion dollar Company "Microsoft".
